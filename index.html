Mahabubul Alam

Hi, I am Mahabubul Alam, an electrical engineering Ph.D. candidate at Pennsylvania State University, University Park (Penn State). I work as a graduate research assistant at the Lab of Green and Secure Integrated Circuit Systems (LOGICS). Dr. Swaroop Ghosh is my supervisor. Currently, I am working on quantum-classical hybrid deep learning models and applications. I am broadly interested in quantum computing, EDA, VLSI, and applied statistical learning.
