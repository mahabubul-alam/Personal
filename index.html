Mahabubul Alam

Hi, I am Mahabubul Alam, an electrical engineering Ph.D. candidate at Pennsylvania State University, University Park (Penn State). I work as a graduate research assistant at the Lab of Green and Secure Integrated Circuit Systems (LOGICS). Dr. Swaroop Ghosh is my supervisor. Currently, I am working on circuit compilation techniques for quantum approximate optimization algorithm. I am broadly interested in quantum computing, EDA, applied statistical learning, and VLSI.
